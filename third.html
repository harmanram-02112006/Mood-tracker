Multimodal Emotion AI: More Data, Better Decisions

Analyze emotions across video, audio, and text to gain insights from multiple channels, ensuring every emotion is accurately captured. Imentiv AI processes facial expressions, vocal tones, and text sentiment for a deeper emotional understanding, tracking emotional shifts across media types for precise insights. Use this data to create emotionally attuned content that truly resonates with your audience

Multiple Face Recognition and Speaker Identification

Detects and recognizes multiple faces in each frame, assigning unique face IDs for tracking. Identifies speakers and segments their speech by time (also known as Speaker Diarization), mapping when each speaker talks. Users can match speakers with faces or analyze them separately. The system delivers continuous facial and audio emotion data for each individual throughout the video.

Emotion Graph

Discover a new dimension of understanding with our AI-driven Emotion Graph. This innovative tool swiftly maps facial emotions onto a dynamic graph, allowing you to comprehend emotions at a glance. Witness frame-by-frame emotions in action as the graph moves in sync with the video, creating an intuitive visual experience.

The Emotion Wheel provides the theoretical foundation, outlining primary emotions. The Emotion Graph, inspired by the Emotion Wheel and functioning as a dynamic counterpart, translates these emotions into a circular visual format. As the video plays, the moving dot on the Emotion Graph corresponds to changing emotions, offering users a real-time and intuitive understanding of facial emotions through facial expressions. This connection between theory and visualization, enhances users' ability to grasp the emotional nuances depicted in videos. Unlock a deeper level of emotional insight and enhance your connections with the power of AI.

Personality Trait Analysis

Our Emotion AI tool offers a comprehensive Personality Trait Analysis for entire videos using the Big Five OCEAN model: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism. This analysis aggregates personality traits across all individuals featured in the video and presents the results in a bar chart. Each trait is color-coded—purple for Openness, green for Conscientiousness, red for Extraversion, yellow for Agreeableness, and blue for Neuroticism—based on established psychological theories.

By analyzing these traits, our tool provides insights into the overall personality profile of the video content, highlighting dominant traits and helping to understand the collective emotional and psychological impact.
